The Quietus Estate - Introduction

Awakened in the eerie silence of a decaying mansion, you find yourself trapped within The Quietus Estate, a sprawling, grandiose home steeped in mystery and ruin. The doors are sealed, the windows impenetrable, and the faint sound of shuffling feet suggests you’re not alone. Once owned by the enigmatic Hawthorne family, the estate’s halls now crawl with undead horrors—victims of the family’s forbidden experiments to conquer death.

To escape, you must navigate the mansion’s labyrinth of grand ballrooms, shadowy corridors, and hidden passages, scavenging scarce resources and solving intricate puzzles to unlock the way out. But the mansion holds more than just the undead—it has a sinister will of its own, and the deeper you delve, the more it seems determined to keep you within its walls forever.

Stealth, strategy, and sharp instincts will be your greatest allies as you uncover the twisted truth of the Hawthornes and fight to break free from the clutches of The Quietus Estate. Will you escape, or will you become one of the restless souls trapped inside?
